About

Chosen topic

This project aims to visualize the world’s life expectancy and it’s relationship with various contributing factors. An important aspect to depict will be the ‘healthy life expectancy’ versus the ‘years lived with disability’, as an important measurement of life quality in general.
The project also visualizes the major causes of death, as a way to spot problems and question the quality of health care systems. The project also aims to spot the existing global inequalities, depict the changes over the years, raise social awareness and provoke change.

Significance of the work

Life expectancy is the key metric for assessing population health. Broader than the narrow metric of the infant and child mortality, which focus solely at mortality at a young age, life expectancy captures the mortality along the entire life course. Understanding the major factors that contribute to causes of death and life expectancy can help us achieve sustained improvements in health for entire populations, increase the healthy, disability-free years of world’s life and erase global inequalities.
